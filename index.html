Scoring Methodology

Mechanism (0–10)

Strength of the biological rationale. Genetic validation in humans, known pathway, published MOA. Demerits for hand-waving about "cellular health" without specifics.

Clinical Stage (0–10)

10 = FDA-approved for any indication. 8 = Phase 2/3 human data. 6 = Phase 1. 4 = Preclinical (NHP). 2 = Mouse only. 0 = No animal data / in vitro only.

Biomarker Relevance (0–10)

Are validated aging biomarkers moving? Epigenetic clocks, p16/p21 senescence markers, NAD+ levels, inflammatory panels (IL-6, TNF-α), telomere length. More validated endpoints = higher score.

Safety Profile (0–10)

10 = Generally recognized as safe, decades of data. 7 = FDA-approved drug with known side effect profile. 4 = Early human safety data. 1 = Theoretical safety concerns (oncogenesis, immunosuppression).

Cost Accessibility (0–10)

10 = Free or <$10/month. 7 = $10–100/month. 4 = $100–500/month. 2 = Requires prescription + monitoring. 0 = Experimental only / not accessible.

Composite Score

Weighted average: Mechanism (25%) + Clinical Stage (25%) + Biomarkers (20%) + Safety (15%) + Cost (15%). Maximum 10.0. Color-coded: green ≥7, yellow 4–7, red <4.
